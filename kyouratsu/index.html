Kyouratsu キョウラツ 公式サイト

    The cute character Kyouratsu was inspired by the logo of Node.js at first, which is a regular hexagon. After having received perspective, it got mixed up with the concept of fractal, so he then had two ears.

    He prefers to be recognized as a cat, for cosplaying the Octocat is really fun. With a Japanese-style name, he feels free to be part in humans' society, though humans' foolishness sometimes annoys him. Anyway he likes being together with humans.
