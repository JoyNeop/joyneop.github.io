<h3>Polytransit</h3>

<p>Transit Cards are issued by corporations which operate under the license of Department of Transportation. The Polytransit brand is created for universal recognition. All public transit services, in which passengers can transfer to un Polytransit-participating service without exit and re-entry, are required by law to participate Polytransit protocol, while they are free to accept other payments. </p>

<h3>Cabin Crew Certificate</h3>

<p>Instead of another passport category, un cabin crew certificate in combination with un ordinary passport is equivalent to a service passport. Upon batch requests from un government-accredited transportation service provider, Civil Transport Services Administration may issue un cabin crew certificate to each designated person. Foreigners are also eligible for cabin crew certificates.</p>
