About

There are vast songs written in various languages, yet there is no Babel for lyrics. People have to search lyrics and their translation from many different websites, and nobody can make sure which version is most correct. Therefore, we are establishing an encyclopedia for lyrics in all languages and maintain translated version in all languages, to make all lyrics accessible for everyone with no barrier.

Join us

We will need volunteers to collect and translate lyrics in all languages by all artists, without any payment. When this website is technically built, we also need people to spread our idea around the Internet, where this project exists significantly. We need your power.
