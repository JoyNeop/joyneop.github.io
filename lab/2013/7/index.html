NeOPSTEP

The round pegs in the square holes.
The ones who see things differently.
We make tools for these kinds of people.

Skeuomorphism.
Our faith.

We insist skeuomorph. We hate flat design.

If Apple betray us, we have to make sound. And NeOPSTEP is our way to protest. Living with a Mac with flat GUI is terrible, this project has thus been launched.

First Step,
Theme For Macintosh.

We will introduce an application which will customize the OS X.

With the application, our skeuomorph design can be installed in OS X and replace evil flat resources.

Second Step,
Rebuild Foundations.

With Mach, FreeBSD, and other wheels, we will get started to implement our own technical foundations. And we will provide one-station solution for users.

It will become a complete OS in a few years.

Third Step,
The Ultimate Haven.

NeOPSTEP will beat down Apple but later versions will continuously be released under both GPLv2 and BSD License, though only designed for Macintosh.

We know both this aim and this project may be utopia, but we won't give up. We won't betray skeuomorphism. We won't let Apple damage the world.

Join Us

Watch our GitHub repository and you can get updated information. We will try our best to preserve skeuomorphic taste and fight against Apple, though it's a long term game.
