The crisis coming

Since the jailbreak of iOS 7 has been released, some things doubtful have emerged. Firstly Cydia is proved not official. Suddenly Chinese users reported that they could not access Cydia and there was a built-in fake Cydia called TaiG. Then a mysterious Chinese company has been described as sponsoring jailbreak hackers to bundle TaiG, a piracy apps delivery system. Now it has been a widespread joke that next WWJC, Worldwide Jailbreak Conference, will be held in China thanks to the sponsor.

In China, TaiG has been proved to be alias of Kuaiyong, a "famous" piracy tool. More evidence will appear here soon.

Fight against evil

We are faced with a common enimy, TaiG, perhaps and other Chinese companies involved, who want to insert their software to our jailbreak tool. What will they do? We can hardly know. They simply want to force Chinese users to use their pirate apps delivery platform? Hard to believe. Why won't they collect users' private information as well, since USA government did so? Hard to refuse such doubt.

Spread our voice

To defend Chinese companies from polluting our jailbreak community, developers' intellegence property, and users' privacy, your voice is needed. Please spread our voice loudly, make more people notice such evil. And protest to the jailbreak hackers and the government. In the United Sates, jailbreaking iPhone is legal unless piracy is bound. We may lose the right to jailbreak iPhone if the hackers keep TaiG bundled with jailbreak tools. Therefore, fighting against such damage is our duty. We want jailbreak community to be clean, and you?
